# Use Case Scenarios

This repository provides practical networking solutions for secure remote management of MikroTik devices. Below are real-world scenarios where these configurations can be applied.

---

## 1. **Homelab Administrator Managing Devices from Anywhere**

**Context**:  
A network enthusiast runs a homelab with 3 MikroTik nodes (including RB941 and CHR on VirtualBox) behind a residential ISP connection (e.g., IndiHome). The ISP uses CGNAT or restricts inbound ports, making direct remote access impossible.

**Solution**:  
- Use **outbound L2TP/IPsec or SSTP** from the MikroTik to a self-hosted VPS or cloud instance.
- Alternatively, establish an **outbound WireGuard tunnel to tunnel.id** (as shown in `/wireguard-tunnelid/`).

## 2. **IT Support Technician Providing Remote Assistance**

**Context**:  
A freelance IT technician needs to troubleshoot client MikroTik routers located in small offices or retail stores with limited IT staff.

**Solution**:  
- Pre-configure the clientâ€™s MikroTik with an **SSTP server** (runs on TCP 443, works behind most firewalls).
- Share temporary VPN credentials to establish a secure session.
- Use Winbox over the encrypted tunnel to diagnose routing, firewall, or bandwidth issues.

**Security Note**:  
- Credentials are rotated after each session.
- Winbox access is restricted to the VPN subnet only (`192.168.99.0/24`).

## 3. **Small Business with Multi-Site Connectivity**

**Solution**:
